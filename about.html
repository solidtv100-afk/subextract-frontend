About Us

YouTube Subtitle Downloader is a simple online tool that helps users extract publicly available English subtitles from YouTube videos.

Our goal is to make learning, research, and content creation easier by providing clean subtitle text in an easy-to-download format.

This tool works only with captions that are publicly accessible on YouTube. We do not host, store, or modify any video or subtitle content.

We believe in simplicity, transparency, and respecting platform rules.
